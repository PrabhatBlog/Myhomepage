About Myself

Hi! my name is Prabhat, I am a Ph.D Research Scholar at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Patna. My advisors are Dr. Asif Ekbal (Associate Professor, IIT Patna) and Dr. Mayank Agarwal (Assistant Professor, IIT Patna).

I primarily work on Peer Review Text Analysis to determine significance of peer reviews. Specifically, I am studying: Can we identify new knowledge from texts? How can we identify relevant and quality literature from the academic clutter? Can we predict with reasonable certainty if a given article falls within the scope of a journal? How can we have an AI as an assistant to the editors and reviewers to fix certain challenges in the current peer review system? Can we comment on the quality of peer reviews leveraging NLP, ML, IR? How can citation contexts aid us to identify the quality of research?

Machine learning

Deep leaning

Natural Language Processing

Artificial Intelligence

My long-term research vision is to develop an AI that can understand human scientific merit to some extent, reduce bias and turnaround time in academic peer review, reduce information overload to the scientists, identify inferior quality work and predatory journals, and accelerate relevant literature discovery to aid the scientists.
